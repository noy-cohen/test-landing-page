About

Built on one idea

Less is more. The best tool is the one that stays out of your way.

We believe the web works best when it's fast, open, and understandable by anyone.

This project is a reminder that you don't need a framework, a bundler, or a CI pipeline to ship something beautiful.

What we stand for

Our values

Three principles that guide every decision.

🧭

Simplicity first

Every line of code should earn its place. If it can be removed without losing value, it should be.

🔓

Radical openness

No black boxes. Everything here is readable, forkable, and free to adapt however you like.

🚀

Ship fast

Perfection is the enemy of done. Start simple, iterate, and trust that progress beats planning.

"Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."
— Antoine de Saint-Exupéry
